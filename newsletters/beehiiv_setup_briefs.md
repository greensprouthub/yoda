## 🔧 WHAT TO DO ON THE WEBSITE (beehiiv.com)

### Step 1 — Create each publication
1. Go to **app.beehiiv.com** → click your profile icon (top right) → **"New Publication"**
2. Fill in the name, tagline, and category
3. Repeat for all 4 (you can switch between publications from the top-left dropdown)

### Step 2 — Connect your custom domain (for each)
1. In each publication → **Settings → Domain**
2. Add your custom domain (e.g. `newsletter.minutewins.com` or just `minutewins.com`)
3. Go to your domain registrar (Namecheap/GoDaddy) and add the CNAME record Beehiiv gives you
4. Wait 5–30 min for propagation

### Step 3 — Set up Welcome Email
1. **Automations → Welcome Email** — paste the welcome copy from below
2. Toggle it ON

### Step 4 — Write your first post
1. **Posts → New Post** — paste the first issue outline from below
2. Save as Draft — don't publish yet until domain is live

### Step 5 — Set send schedule
1. **Settings → General** → set your sending cadence preference
2. Posts can be scheduled per-post when you hit "Schedule"
