# 🎋 The Lucky 7 System
### Baris Guler's Personal Operating System
*Built by a neurodivergent solopreneur who needed a system that actually worked.*

---

## What Is It?

The Lucky 7 System is a daily + weekly personal productivity and life management framework designed specifically for ADHD-friendly execution. It maps directly onto the **Puzzle4Life Growth Set** — 7 hexagonal pieces + Star + Heart — making it both a personal framework AND a physical product.

The core idea: **small daily practices compound into big results**. No massive willpower required. Just 7 simple pillars, lived daily.

---

## 🧩 The 9 Pieces

### ⭐ STAR — Continuous Growth & Open Mindset
*Your North Star. The Curiosity Feed.*
- Stay open to new ideas, tools, and perspectives
- Feed your brain daily with what excites you
- Automation: Daily Curiosity Feed (WhatsApp digest of GSH-relevant news)

### ❤️ HEART — Self-Compassion & Start Imperfectly
*The permission piece. Done beats perfect.*
- Start before you're ready
- Embrace discomfort as growth
- Never miss twice — but forgive the first miss

---

### Hex 1 — 💡 Idea Parking Lot
*Capture everything, judge nothing.*
- Every spark, every "what if", every random idea goes here immediately
- No filtering at capture time — just park it
- Weekly: review and promote worthy ideas to Tasks or Deadlines
- **Entity:** IdeaParkingLot
- **Automation:** None needed — always open, always receiving

### Hex 2 — ⚡ Micro-Task Engine
*Break it down, make it doable.*
- Any task that feels overwhelming gets broken into micro-steps (15 min or less each)
- ADHD brain needs small wins to build momentum
- **Entity:** Task (with micro_steps field)
- **Automation:** Morning check-in asks for today's #1 micro-task

### Hex 3 — 🌅 Daily Focus Rhythm
*Morning intention, evening reflection.*
- Morning: set one clear focus for the day
- Evening: reflect on what moved, what didn't
- **Automation:** Morning Check-in (8:15am CT weekdays) + Evening Wind-down (9:30pm CT)

### Hex 4 — ⏰ Deadline Tracker
*Nothing slips, everything visible.*
- Every commitment, deadline, and time-sensitive item lives here
- Daily watchdog scans for anything due in 7 days
- **Entity:** Deadline
- **Automation:** Deadline Watchdog (daily 8am CT) — WhatsApp alert if anything is close

### Hex 5 — 🤝 Accountability Check-ins
*Honest scoreboard, no hiding.*
- Regular check-ins that keep you honest with yourself
- Not punitive — compassionate but direct
- **Automation:** Weekend Check-in (Saturday 8:30am CT), Weekly Accountability Summary (Sunday 8am CT)

### Hex 6 — 🏆 Minute Wins + Habit Stack
*Small daily victories compound.*
- Log at least one "minute win" per day — anything counts
- Stack new habits onto existing ones (morning routine anchor)
- **Entity:** HabitLog (tracks meditation, fitness, dog walk, healthy eating, mood, focus score)
- **Automation:** Evening Wind-down prompts habit log entry

### Hex 7 — 🪞 Daily Reflection & Adjustments
*End of day recalibration. Course correct in real time.*
- What worked today? What didn't?
- One small adjustment for tomorrow
- Part of the Evening Wind-down automation

---

## 🔭 BONUS — Weekly Business Ops Review
*Zoom out. Bigger picture. Plan ahead.*
- Every Sunday evening: review wins, what didn't work, top 3 priorities for next week
- Track Wormspire sales, Puzzle4Life progress, GSH progress
- Rate your energy + overall week
- **Entity:** WeeklyOpsReview
- **Automation:** Weekly Ops Review (Sunday 7pm CT)
