## STEP 1 — Capture and Refine Your Idea with ChatGPT

**Time: 30–60 minutes**
**Tool: ChatGPT (GPT-4)**

Most ideas die in the "vague" phase. The first job is to make it concrete.

### How to do it

Open ChatGPT and paste this prompt:

```
I have a business idea: [YOUR IDEA IN 1-2 SENTENCES].

Help me:
1. Identify the target customer (be specific — age, job, pain)
2. Name the core problem this solves
3. Describe the transformation (before vs after)
4. List 3 competing solutions that already exist
5. Identify what makes this different
6. Suggest 5 possible brand names (short, memorable, .com available)
7. Write a one-sentence value proposition
```

### Example output (real GSH example)

**Idea:** A vertical worm composting tower for apartment gardeners
**Target:** Urban renters 28–45 who want to compost but have no yard
**Problem:** Traditional compost bins smell, attract pests, and need outdoor space
**Transformation:** From throwing food scraps in the trash → turning them into garden gold in 30 days, indoors
**Differentiation:** Vertical, stackable, odor-controlled, fits under a kitchen counter
**Brand names generated:** Wormspire, CastleCast, VermiStack, WormUp, TowerCompost

### What to save

Copy the full ChatGPT output into a Google Doc or Notion. This becomes your brand brief.

## STEP 2 — Generate the AI Build Prompt

**Time: 15–20 minutes**
**Tool: ChatGPT → Base44**

Once your idea is refined, you need to convert it into a precise AI build prompt. Don't wing this — a good prompt is the difference between a generic site and something that actually converts.

### Prompt to generate your build prompt

Paste this into ChatGPT:

```
Based on this brand brief:

- Brand name: [NAME]
- Target customer: [DESCRIPTION]
- Core problem: [PROBLEM]
- Core transformation: [BEFORE → AFTER]
- Tone: [e.g., warm and practical / bold and urgent / calm and trustworthy]

Write a detailed AI app/website build prompt I can paste into Base44 or Lovable 
to generate a full landing page. Include:
- Hero section with headline and subheadline
- Problem section (3 pain points)
- Solution section (3 benefits)
- Social proof placeholder (testimonials)
- Lead capture form (name + email + biggest challenge)
- CTA button copy
- Color palette suggestion (hex codes)
- Font style suggestion
```

### What you get

A ready-to-paste prompt. Copy it exactly.

## STEP 3 — Build the Website in Base44 or Lovable

**Time: 1–2 hours**
**Tools: Base44 (base44.com) or Lovable (lovable.dev)**

### Option A: Base44 (recommended for AI agent + database)

1. Go to **app.base44.com**
2. Click **New App**
3. Paste your full build prompt from Step 2
4. Base44 generates your site with pages, forms, and a live backend
5. Your lead capture form automatically saves submissions to a database
6. No code needed — but you can edit any component

**Best for:** Sites that need a contact form, waitlist, lead capture, or AI agent built in

### Option B: Lovable (recommended for visual polish)

1. Go to **lovable.dev**
2. Click **New Project**
3. Paste your build prompt
4. Lovable generates a React-based site with beautiful UI
5. Connect to Supabase for data storage
6. Export to GitHub when ready

**Best for:** Marketing landing pages where visual design is the priority

### Tips for both platforms

- If the first output isn't quite right, don't rebuild — refine. Type: *"Change the hero headline to be more urgent. Target audience is engineers who fear AI will take their job."*
- Always ask it to add a lead capture form with at least: Name, Email, Biggest Challenge
- Request a mobile-responsive layout explicitly

## STEP 4 — Find and Register Your Domain

**Time: 20–30 minutes**
**Tools: GoDaddy + ChatGPT**

### Step 4a — Generate domain ideas

Paste this into ChatGPT:

```
Brand name: [NAME]
Brand idea: [ONE SENTENCE]

Suggest 10 domain name options across these formats:
- Exact match: brandname.com
- .ai extension: brandname.ai
- Action prefix: getbrandname.com, trybrandname.com, startbrandname.com
- Niche extension: brandname.io, brandname.co, brandname.app

Prioritize short (under 15 characters), easy to spell, and memorable.
```

### Step 4b — Check availability on GoDaddy

1. Go to **godaddy.com**
2. Search each domain suggestion
3. Look for .com first — if taken, .ai is the premium alternative
4. Register for 2 years minimum (~$25–40 total)

### Step 4c — Separate brand from registration

**Important:** Keep your brand identity separate from your domain registrar.

- **GoDaddy** = just the domain registration
- **Base44 or Lovable** = where the website lives
- **Your email** = use Google Workspace ($6/mo) for professional email (you@yourbrand.com)

Point your GoDaddy domain to your Base44 or Lovable site using DNS settings (both platforms provide step-by-step instructions).

## STEP 5 — Create Your Logo

**Time: 30–45 minutes**
**Tools: ChatGPT → Base44 Image Generator or Midjourney**

### Generate a logo prompt

Paste this into ChatGPT:

```
Brand name: [NAME]
Brand vibe: [e.g., earthy and organic / modern and tech / bold and energetic]
Target audience: [DESCRIPTION]
Core product: [WHAT IT IS]

Write a detailed logo generation prompt for an AI image generator. Include:
- Icon style (minimal, flat, line art, etc.)
- Color palette (suggest 2-3 hex codes)
- Typography style
- What the icon should represent visually
- What to avoid
```

### Generate the logo

**Option A — Base44 Image Generator**
- Use the prompt directly in your Base44 agent: "Generate a logo for [brand] — [paste prompt]"

**Option B — Midjourney or DALL-E**
- Paste the prompt with the prefix: *"Professional logo design, vector style, white background —"*

### Refine and finalize

- Generate 4–6 variations
- Pick your favorite
- Ask ChatGPT: *"The logo looks good but the icon feels too generic. How do I make it more specific to [worm composting / puzzle learning / laser engraving]?"*
- Regenerate until you love it

### Export formats needed

- PNG with transparent background (for website)
- Square version (for social media profile)
- Favicon version (32×32px, for browser tab)

## STEP 6 — Run the Fix My Visibility Audit

**Time: 30–45 minutes**
**Tool: Fix My Visibility (GreenSproutHub.ai tool)**

Before you announce anything, audit your site. You don't get a second chance at a first impression.

### What Fix My Visibility checks

- ⚡ Page load speed (should be under 3 seconds)
- 📱 Mobile responsiveness score
- 🔍 SEO basics (title tag, meta description, H1 structure)
- 🔒 Security (HTTPS, SSL certificate active)
- 🖼 Image optimization (are images compressed?)
- 📋 Form functionality (does lead capture actually work?)
- 🔗 Broken links

### How to use it

1. Go to the Fix My Visibility tool (link from GreenSproutHub.ai)
2. Enter your new website URL
3. Run the full audit
4. Download the PDF report

### How to fix the issues

Paste the audit report into ChatGPT or your Base44 agent:

```
Here is my website audit report: [paste report]

I built this site on [Base44 / Lovable]. 
For each issue flagged, tell me exactly how to fix it — 
step by step, no code experience assumed.
```

Implement the fixes, re-run the audit. Aim for a green score before launch.

## STEP 7 — Launch and Announce

**Time: 45–60 minutes**
**Tools: ChatGPT → Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Reddit**

### Generate your launch posts

Paste this into ChatGPT:

```
I just launched a new brand called [NAME].

Here's the context:
- What it does: [ONE SENTENCE]
- Who it's for: [TARGET CUSTOMER]
- Core transformation: [BEFORE → AFTER]
- Website: [URL]

Write launch posts for:
1. Facebook (personal, story-driven, 150 words)
2. Instagram caption (punchy, 3 lines + 5 hashtags)
3. LinkedIn (professional, founder story angle, 200 words)
4. Reddit (community-first, not salesy, for [relevant subreddit])
5. WhatsApp broadcast (casual, short, direct CTA)

Tone: [warm and human / bold and direct / curious and inviting]
```

### Posting schedule (Day 1 launch)

| Time | Platform | Content |
|------|----------|---------|
| 9 AM | LinkedIn | Founder story post |
| 11 AM | Facebook personal | Story + link |
| 12 PM | Instagram | Visual + caption |
| 2 PM | Reddit | Community-first post |
| 6 PM | WhatsApp broadcast | Short + CTA |

### The magic sentence

Every post needs this somewhere:

> *"I built this in [X days] using AI tools — here's how, and here's the link."*

People are obsessed with the process right now. Built-in-public always outperforms polished marketing.

## Bonus: The Hormozi Lead Magnet Layer

Once your site is live, add a lead magnet immediately. Don't just ask people to "contact you."

### The 3 types that work (from Alex Hormozi)

**Type 1 — Reveal a problem they didn't know they had**
> Example: "Is your website invisible? Run our free visibility scan." → Fix My Visibility audit

**Type 2 — Free trial or sample**
> Example: "Get one free Puzzle4Life piece — just pay $4.99 shipping" → they touch the product, they want the set

**Type 3 — One step of many**
> Example: "Download the free LLC checklist — step 1 of your business setup" → upsell to the full back-office bundle

### Naming formula (Hormozi)

*"[Number] + [Specific Outcome] + [Timeframe]"*

Examples:
- "3 Signs Your Compost Bin Is Failing (And How to Fix It This Weekend)"
- "How to Go From Employee to Founder in 90 Days Without Quitting Your Job Yet"
- "5 Website Mistakes Killing Your Local Business (Free Scan)"
